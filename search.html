    Toronto is the capital city of Ontario and is the largest city in Canada with a population of over 6 million people currently. Toronto is a centre for business, arts, culture and is recognized as one of the most multicultural cosmopolitan cities in the world. The current mayor of Toronto is John Tory and has been in office since 2014. Toronto is also home to the University of Toronto, one of the most prestigious research universities in Canada. In 2021, The Economist’s Safe City Index ranked Toronto 2nd safest city in the world.
